First Class Service

Malones are experts in supplying and maintaining Australian medical equipment:

  • Washer-Disinfectors, Autoclaves, Bedpan Washers, Sterilisers, Laboratory Washers, Utensil Washers Automation Robotics and CSSD equipment.
  • Ultrasonic Cleaners, Operating Tables, Blanket Warmers, Endoscope Washers, Reverse Osmosis Systems, Steam Generators and Boilers.

We are ISO 9001 certified and comply with Australian Standards AS 5369 (formerly AS/NZS 4187) for Reprocessing of Reusable Medical Devices and with AS/NZS 3551 for Management Programs for Medical Equipment.
