KEN BWD 731

Water and power efficient washer-disinfector for clinics, hospitals, and nursing homes requiring full process documentation that exceeds AS 2437 and AS 5369 (formerly AS/NZS 4187).

Capacity
  • 1 bedpan OR
  • 2 urine bottles OR
  • 1 washing bowl OR
  • 1 utensil basket

Wall hung, floor mount on plinth, or under bench installation. Automatic door doubles as a slop sink. Accessories and racks available.
